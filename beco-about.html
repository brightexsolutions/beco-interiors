About Beco

A material partner built for interior teams who know what they need — and need it now.

Our Story

Kenya's trusted
interior materials partner

Beco Interiors is a Nairobi-based specialist supplier of premium building and interior finishing materials, serving architects, interior designers, contractors, and developers who demand quality they can rely on.

Located at Urban Square in Nairobi's Industrial Area, our showroom stocks over 520 products across four core categories — sintered stone, lighting, WPC wall panels, and accessories. We make specification easier, quoting faster, and sourcing simpler.

How We Work

A process built around
your project flow

01
Discover & Specify

Browse our full range online or visit the showroom in person. Our team is available to assist with material selection and specification for residential, commercial, and hospitality projects.

02
Shortlist & Sample

Narrow your selection by finish, application, and budget. Samples can be viewed in the showroom, and our team provides guidance on what works best together.

03
Request a Quote

Submit your cart online or contact us directly. We prepare a formal quote with pricing, availability, and lead times within 2 business hours for stocked items.

04
Collect or Arrange Delivery

Confirm, pay, and collect from Urban Square — or arrange delivery to your project site in Nairobi. We work around your schedule, not a generic ecommerce checkout.
