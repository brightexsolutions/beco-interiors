07 — Voice & Tone
 Write Like This
  • Confident and premium — without sounding inflated
  • Specific: say KSh 850 not "affordable"; say 2 hours not "fast"
  • CTA verbs go to the destination: "Shop Lighting →" not "Click here"
  • Warm and Kenyan in the About and Contact pages
 Avoid This
  • Generic filler: "world-class", "cutting-edge", "transformative"
  • Weak CTAs: "Click here", "Learn more" (without context)
  • Overcrowded headings — one strong idea per heading
  • Placeholder pages and dead nav links (rebuild trust)
